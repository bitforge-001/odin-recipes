Pizza

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made of a flat round bread base topped with flavorful ingredients and baked to perfection. The base, known as the crust, can be thin and crispy or thick and soft. A layer of rich tomato sauce is spread over it, followed by a generous amount of melted cheese—usually mozzarella. Different toppings are added based on taste: vegetables like onions, capsicum, olives, tomatoes, and mushrooms; or meats like pepperoni, chicken, and sausage. When baked in a hot oven, the cheese melts beautifully, creating a golden, savory surface.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (e.g., pepperoni, bell peppers, onions, mushrooms)
  • Oregano and chilli flakes

Steps:

  1. Prepare the dough by mixing flour, yeast, water, and a pinch of salt. Knead until smooth and let it rise for about an hour.
  2. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature (usually around 475-500°F or 245-260°C).
  3. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  4. Spread a layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  5. Add a generous layer of mozzarella cheese.
  6. Add your favorite toppings evenly over the cheese.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  8. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve hot. Enjoy your homemade pizza!
